Research

Members study corporate purpose from many angles. These are the themes that run through the group's work. A selection of publications will be added here as they appear.

Theme

Does purpose work?

Whether and when corporate purpose shapes firm performance and resilience — including how purpose-driven organizations weather crises and periods of polycrisis.

Theme

Purpose for whom?

How purpose is experienced by employees and stakeholders, and what it means in different organizational settings such as family firms.

Theme

Implementing purpose

How organizations put purpose into practice — the practices, levers, and maturity that turn stated purpose into organizational change.

Theme

Legitimacy & foundations

The conceptual and moral foundations of corporate purpose: how it is defined, justified, and legitimated, and the tensions this involves.

Theme

Purpose & profit after the backlash

What remains of the purpose agenda amid ESG scrutiny, geopolitical fragmentation, and a more contested environment for stakeholder commitments.

Theme

Purpose in the age of AI

How purpose and leadership are reshaped as technology and artificial intelligence change how organizations create value.

Purpose Library

A public library of talks, reports, and materials on corporate purpose, curated by the HEC Paris S&O Purpose Center.
